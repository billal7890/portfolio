Leadership & Program Development

From Data to Decision: Analytics & Storytelling Workshop

A four-hour professional development workshop organized through the BAIM Graduate Student Association in collaboration with the University of Delaware Library's Research Data & Design Commons.

April 24, 2026Morris LibraryBAIM GSA

My role as organizer and event coordinator

I developed the workshop concept and learning objectives, coordinated with the department and library team, secured the Data Analysis & Visualization Lab, managed registration and communications, selected hands-on datasets, supported event logistics, and facilitated the student experience.

The workshop guided participants through data ethics, storytelling with data, data management, and practical analysis. Students applied the workflow to real-world datasets, shared their findings, and gained an opportunity to earn a LinkedIn-shareable badge recognizing their participation and professional development.
